To Solve Game:

1. Collect flashlight from Statue in Room A
2. Enter Room B
3. Check Carpet to collect Key
4. Check the statue, "Venus de Milo"
5. Check out Room C
6. Walk up to the bench
7. Check the office
8. Check the desk to your right
9. Check the bottom drawer. Collect Screwdriver
10. Check the office
11. Check out Room C
12. Walk up to the bench
13. Check the Painting
14. Remove 'La Belle Jardiniere'
15. Walk up to the bench
16. Walk into Room B
17. Walk into Room A
18. Walk up to the window.
